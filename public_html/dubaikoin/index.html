About us

One of the leading ventures to support innovative and disruptive ideas in the blockchain space. Since 2013, Boundless team members have been avid supporters of blockchain technology. We have scouted almost all credible projects that entered the blockchain space in the past years and have seen their growth closer than most crypto ventures.

Our experience of almost 10+ years in the crypto-space allows us to guide new ambitious projects towards a successful path. The boundless team makes sure that our suggestions and advice are derived from real-world examples of several other projects to provide a basis for our claims of success or failure for the respective projects.

Besides financial advisory, Boundless feels proud to claim itself as a one-stop shop for new founders to find investors for their projects. Whether it's us who provide the funding or other investors, we make sure you can find the perfect match for your business needs.
